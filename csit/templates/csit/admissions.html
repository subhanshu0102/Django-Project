Admissions

CSIT,Durg is pioneer technical institute offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in several streams of Engineering. We have campus with over 3000 students, coached by distinguished faculty using the latest teaching aids. The campuse have excellent hostel facilities and a vision of producing engineers of international standards. We also aware the students about their responsibility towards community and national development. This is what distinguishes us from the run-of-the-mill engineering institutes spread all over the city.
